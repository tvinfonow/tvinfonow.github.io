Brianne Leary Biography

Brianne Leary is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on July 28, 1957 in Providence, Rhode Island, United States. Brianne Banigan Leary (born July 28th 1957) is an American actor and inventor. She hosted the second season of Disney Channel’s of Walt Disney World Inside Out alongside George Foreman and J.D. Roth. She was also the host of the Animal Planet’s Petsburgh USA.

She also developed the Canadian children’s TV program,”Stickin’ Around, with Robin Steele.

Her first performance was that of the nurse Susan Ames in the series Baa Baa Black Sheep (1977-1978). Later, she reprised her role as the officer Sindy Cahill during the 2nd season of CHiPs (1978-1979), before appearing as a guest on several shows, such as Buck Rogers in the 25th Century; The Fall Guy; Simon & Simon; No Soap, Radio; as Russian gymnast Tania in The Paper Chase episode “A Case of Detente” in 1979 and 1st & Ten. Also, she appeared in Battle of the Network Stars V in November 1978, and Battle of the Network Stars VI in May 1979.
