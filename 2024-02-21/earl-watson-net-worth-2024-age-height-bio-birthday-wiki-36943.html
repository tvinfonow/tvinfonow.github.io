Earl Watson Biography

Earl Watson is one of the most popular and richest Basketball Player who was born on September 14, 1990 in Fort Pierce, Fort Pierce, United States. Earl Watson (born September 14, 1990) is an American professional basketball player for Fundación Granada of the LEB Oro. Standing at 2.01 m (6’7″), he plays both the power forward and the center positions. After two years at Chipola Junior College and two years at Rhode Island Watson entered the 2016 NBA draft but was not selected in the draft’s two rounds.

On July 31, 2017, Watson joined SCM CSU Craiova of the Romanian League. On November 23, 2017, he left Craiova and joined IRT Tanger in the Nationale 1. On August 26, 2018, Watson signed with Río Ourense Termal of the LEB Oro.
