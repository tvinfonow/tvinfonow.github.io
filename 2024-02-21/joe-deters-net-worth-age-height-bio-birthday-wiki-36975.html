Joe Deters Biography

Joe Deters is one of the most popular and richest Lawyer who was born on April 4, 1957 in United States.

Deters is a 1979 graduate of the University of Cincinnati and graduated from the University of Cincinnati College Of Law in 1982. He is a member of the Cincinnati Bar Association, National District Attorneys Association and Ohio Prosecuting Attorneys Association. He has served on the University of Cincinnati Board of Trustees, Ohio Organized Crime Commission and the Southern Ohio Leukemia Foundation.

Deters began his career in public service in 1982 as an Assistant Hamilton County Prosecutor. In 1988 after six years as an Assistant Prosecutor, Deters was elected the Hamilton County Clerk of Courts where he developed skills necessary to manage a large office. In 1992, Deters returned to the Hamilton County Prosecutor’s office when he was appointed and later elected Prosecuting Attorney. Deters started the first victim and witness advocate program in Hamilton County. He was subsequently reelected in 1996.
