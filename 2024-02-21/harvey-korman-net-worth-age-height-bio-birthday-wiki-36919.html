Top Facts about Harvey Korman

  • Harvey Korman was an American actor and comedian born in 1927.

  • He also starred in the sitcom “The Harvey Korman Show” in 1978.
  • Korman passed away at the age of 81 in 2008 due to complications from a ruptured abdominal aortic aneurysm.
