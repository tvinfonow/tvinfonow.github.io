Joe Sumner Biography

Joe Sumner is one of the most popular and richest Rock Singer who was born on November 23, 1976 in England, United Kingdom. Popular as the lead vocalist and bassist of the British rock group Fiction Plane, this musician released several albums which include Everything Will Never Be OK and Left Side of the Brain.

He as well as English musician Dan Grech Marguerat were both involved at different times throughout their professional careers with RAK Studios in London.

Fiction Plane recorded its first album, Everything Will Never Be OK (2003), without a full-time drummer, relying instead on session musician Abe Laboriel Jr. Soon after, Fiction Plane hired drummer Pete Wilhoit, a native of Indiana.

After that album, Fiction Plane released Bitter Forces and Lame Race Horses (2005), Left Side of the Brain (2007), Paradiso (2009), Sparks (2010), and Mondo Lumina (2015). The band received much attention when it was the opening act for The Police’s reunion tour in 2007.

A son of Northern Irish television actress Frances Tomelty and world-renowned British singer Sting was raised with his family in Britain. United Kingdom.
