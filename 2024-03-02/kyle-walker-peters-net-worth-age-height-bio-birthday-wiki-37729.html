Kyle Walker-Peters Biography

Kyle Walker-Peters is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on April 13, 1997 in Edmonton, Edmonton, United Kingdom. Kyle Leonardus Walker-Peters (born 13 April 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defender for Premier League club Southampton, on loan from Tottenham Hotspur.

On 26 December 2018, Walker-Peters successfully registered three assists during a 5–0 home win against Bournemouth, becoming the youngest player, aged 21 years and 257 days, to provide three assists in a Premier League game since Jermaine Pennant (aged 20 years and 227 days) made three in August 2003 for Leeds United against Middlesbrough.

At the start of the 2017–18 season Walker-Peters made his full Premier League debut against Newcastle United helping to earn Tottenham a 2–0 away victory at St James’ Park. Sky Sports went on to award him Man of the Match. On 28 February 2018, he scored his first goal for Tottenham in the 6–1 win against Rochdale in the fifth round FA Cup tie.

On 29 January 2020, Walker-Peters joined fellow Premier League side Southampton on loan until the end of the season.

In the 2018–19 season, Walker-Peters made his first start of the season on 31 October 2017 in the EFL Cup tie against West Ham United that Tottenham won 3–1. He also had his first appearance in the Premier League this season as a substitute in game against Leicester City.
