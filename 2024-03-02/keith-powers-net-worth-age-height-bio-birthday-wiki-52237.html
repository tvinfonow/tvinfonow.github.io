Keith Powers Biography

Keith Powers is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on August 22, 1992 in Sacramento, California, United States. Model turned actor who starred as Cameron Drake on Netflix’s #REALITYHIGH and played Theo on the MTV series Faking It. He’s also been in Famous in Love and The New Edition Story. He previously worked as a model with brands such as Calvin Klein, GQ, Guess, and Sketchers, among others.

He starred on Faking it alongside Gregg Sulkin.

After graduating in 2010, he moved to Los Angeles with his father to focus on modeling, signing with the Wilhelmina Agency. He walked for Calvin Klein at the 2014 Men’s Fashion Week in Milan, modeled in Sears and JCPenny ads, did a cover for Skechers, and became the face of Aéropostale.

In 2013 Powers landed a role in the movie House Party: Tonight’s the Night. After flying to South Africa for shooting, he fell in love with acting and decided to make it his main occupation. He played Gutta in the BET hip hop anthology series Tales, and Cameron Drake in the Netflix original film Reality High. He had a recurring role on the MTV series Faking It and has since become known for his part as Tyree in the film Straight Outta Compton. He was cast as Ronnie DeVoe in The New Edition Story and since April 2017 has co-starred in Famous in Love on Freeform. He also stars in the Netflix series What/If (2019).

He has been in a relationship with singer Ryan Destiny since 2017.
