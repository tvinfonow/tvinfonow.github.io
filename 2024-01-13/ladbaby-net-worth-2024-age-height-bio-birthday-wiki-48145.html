On 13 December 2019, the Hoyle couple released a single entitled “I Love Sausage Rolls”, a parody cover of Alan Merrill’s “I Love Rock ‘n’ Roll”. The song became the Christmas number one, beating the likes of Stormzy (#2), Lewis Capaldi (#3) and Dua Lipa (#4), and spent one week in the top 40.
