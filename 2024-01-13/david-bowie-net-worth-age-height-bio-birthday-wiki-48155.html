Top Facts about David Bowie

  • Born David Robert Jones in London, England in 1947.
  • Released his first album, “David Bowie,” in 1967.
  • Achieved commercial success with the single “Space Oddity” (1969).
  • Ziggy Stardust persona became a cultural icon in the early 1970s.
  • Sold over 140 million records worldwide.
  • Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1996.
  • Won six Grammy Awards throughout his career.
  • Acted in films such as “Labyrinth” (1986) and “The Prestige” (2006).
  • Died from liver cancer on January 10, 2016 at age 69.
  • Continues to influence music and fashion today
