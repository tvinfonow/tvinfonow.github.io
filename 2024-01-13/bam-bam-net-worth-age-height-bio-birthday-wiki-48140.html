Bam Bam Biography

Bam Bam is one of the most popular and richest Radio Personality who was born on January 2, 1970 in Chester, United Kingdom. Peter Jarrod Poulton (born 2 January 1970) popularly referred to in the media as Bam Bam and also known as Peter Jarrod is a British radio host. He was born in Chester, Poulton has lived in the London Borough of Barnet with his partner Emma and their two children since 2003.

Between September 2006 to November 2007 Poulton as well as Rob Hayes worked together on an audio podcast hosted by THESHOW.com. It was originally titled”Bam Bam Daily “Bam Bam Daily” and later the “Bam Bam Show”, the program could be downloaded each day in MP3 format. It contained content that could not be broadcast in normal radio stations, like “The Bam Bam Answer phone”, “Kat Shoob World News”,. and a variety of pranks performed by Hayes as well as a variety of talks with Hari Krishna. The show also featured regular contributions by James Max (The Apprentice and LBC) and Council Dweller Dave.

In March 2007, Poulton joined London’s Capital 95.8 and presented the station’s evening show Monday to Friday with co-hosts Rob Hayes and Kat Shoob. On 6 December 2007, Radio Today reported that Poulton had left Capital. In its nine-month run, his show increased the number of listeners to Capital Radio, during its broadcast hours, by 55%.

On 3 August 2008, as Peter Jarrod, he returned to Virgin Radio to cover for Iain Lee and Geoff Lloyd. The show used the same music format as Poulton’s former Capital show.

In January 1999, he was later a presenter of his Bam Bam Breakfast on London’s Kiss 100 with co-host Rob Hayes who is also called Streetboy. The show was hosted by him for seven years as the very first host host to gain market leadership over the dominating Chris Tarrant at Capital Breakfast with a demographic of 15-24. The show was awarded multiple Sony Radio Academy Awards, in addition to New York Festival and CRCA industry awards.
