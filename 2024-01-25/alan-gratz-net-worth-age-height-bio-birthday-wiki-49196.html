Alan Gratz Biography

Alan Gratz is one of the most popular and richest Young Adult Author who was born on January 27, 1972 in Tennessee, Tennessee, United States. Notable for young adult novels such as Something Rotten (2007), Fantasy Baseball (2011), Prisoner B-3087 (2013), and The Dragon Lantern: A League of Seven Novel (2015), Gratz is also recognized for his television writing work for the A&E series City Confidential.

In 2004, Gratz adapted Washington Irving‘s famous short story “The Legend of Sleepy Hollow” into a stage play that premiered at the Knoxville Actors Co-Op.

He was born and raised in Knoxville, Tennessee, and he later settled in the western region of North Carolina.
