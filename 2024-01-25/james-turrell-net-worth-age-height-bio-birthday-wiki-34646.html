James Turrell Biography

James Turrell is one of the most popular and richest Painter who was born on May 6, 1943 in Pasadena, California, United States. He is best known for his large-scale art installation at Flagstaff’s Roden Crater. Turrell is also well-known for his Stuck Red/Stuck Blue installation as well as his Aten Reign light projections.

He worked with Japanese architect Tadao to create a sculpture called the Minamidera.

As the son of Archibald Milton Turrell, an engineer and physician, he grew up in Pasadena. He was raised Quaker.
