Mario Cuomo Biography

Mario Cuomo is one of the most popular and richest Politician who was born on June 15, 1932 in New York City, New York State, United States. American politician and Democrat who, between 1975 and 1994, served as New York’s Secretary of State, Lieutenant Governor, and Governor. He came to national prominence after giving the keynote speech at the 1984 Democratic National Convention in which he criticized the policies of then-President Ronald Reagan.

He and David Paterson were both Governors of New York.

He was married for more than fifty years to his wife Matilda, whom he had five children with.
