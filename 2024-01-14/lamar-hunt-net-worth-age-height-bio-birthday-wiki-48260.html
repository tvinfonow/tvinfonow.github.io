Lamar Hunt Biography

Lamar Hunt is one of the most popular and richest Sports Executive who was born on August 2, 1932 in El Dorado, Arkansas, United States. American sports executive who was partly responsible for the founding of the American Football League, Major League Soccer and World Championship Tennis. He is a Hall of Famer in all three sports and is the namesake of the NFL’s Lamar Hunt Trophy, given to the winner of the American Football Conference each year.

He founded the NFL’s Kansas City Chiefs and hired the team’s first head coach, Hank Stram.

His father is oil tycoon H.L. Hunt. He was married to Norma Hunt, his second wife, for 42 years and had children named Clark, Daniel and Sharron.
