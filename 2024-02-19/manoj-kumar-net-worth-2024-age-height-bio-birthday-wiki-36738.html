Manoj Kumar Biography

Manoj Kumar is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on July 24, 1937 in Abbottabad, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. He is a veteran leading Indian actor and director, who was awarded the Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award for 1999. He won seven out of 13 Filmfare Awards that he was nominated, from 1968 to 1976.

In 1999, he was awarded the Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award. The award was won by Hema Mlini the next year.

Kumar represented Fiji in the most Oceania championships: 2001; 2007 Nadi, Fiji; 2009 Gold Coast; and 2011 Rotoura, New Zealand. Kumar also represented Fiji in three Chess Olympiads.

Kumar won the first Fiji Invitational Championship in 2003.

With his wife Shashi Goswami he had two sons, Vishal, and Kunal. He also has a brother, Rajiv.
