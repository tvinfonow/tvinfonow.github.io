Jazmyn Simon Biography

Jazmyn Simon is one of the most popular and richest TV Actress who was born on December 30, 1984 in San Francisco, California, United States. Most well known for her portrayal of Julie Greane on the HBO series Ballers. She also stars as Trixie in the Adult Swim movie The Hindenburg Explodes! and plays Kat Neese on the Netflix series Raising Dion.

In Ballers, her onscreen husband, Charles Greane, is played by Omar Benson Miller.

She has gained attention for her relationship with former Psyche star Dule Hill. They became engaged on April 14, 2017 and married in early 2018. In May 2019, they welcomed a son named Levi together.
