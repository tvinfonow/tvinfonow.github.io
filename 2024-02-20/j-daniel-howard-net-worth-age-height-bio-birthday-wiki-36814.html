J Daniel Howard Biography

J Daniel Howard is one of the most popular and richest Politician who was born on August 24, 1943 in Tennessee, United States. After living and working in Japan, Poland, and Cyprus as a United States Foreign Service official, Howard became both United States Assistant Secretary of Defense for Public Affairs and Under Secretary of the Navy.

During the presidency of Ronald Reagan, Howard was given the titles of White House Deputy Press Secretary for Foreign Affairs and Special Assistant to the President.

He was born and raised in Chattanooga, Tennessee.
