Agnes Moorehead Biography

Agnes Moorehead is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on December 6, 1900 in Clinton, Massachusetts, United States. Actress who played the mother, Endora, on the TV series Bewitched. She won Golden Globe Awards for Best Supporting Actress for her roles in the 1945 film Mrs. Tarkington and the 1965 film Hush…Hush, Sweet Charlotte.

She appeared in the 1941 film, Citizen Kane, which was directed by its star, Orson Welles.

She married John Griffith Lee in 1930, and the couple divorced twenty-two years later.
