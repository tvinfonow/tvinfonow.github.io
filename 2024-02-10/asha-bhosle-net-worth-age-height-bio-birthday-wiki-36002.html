Asha Bhosle Biography

Asha Bhosle is one of the most popular and richest World Music Singer who was born on September 8, 1933 in Sangli, India. Known as Ashaji, she is a Bollywood playback singer who has contributed music to more than one thousand films.

She has worked with composer A.R. Rahman.

She had a friendly sibling rivalry going with her sister and fellow singer Lata Mangeshkar. She was married to Rahul Dev Burman from 1980 until 1994.
