Wyatt Rice Biography

Wyatt Rice is one of the most popular and richest Singer who was born on January 6, 1965 in Long Beach, United States. The year was 1990. Rice made his first solo release with all instruments New Market Gap on the Rounder Records label with help from Rickie Simpkins (fiddle), Sammy Shelor (banjo), Ray Legere (mandolin) as well as Ron Rice (bass).

Rice became part of Ronnie Bowman’s group The Committee in 2003 along with Andy Hall (resonator guitar), Jeremy Garrett (fiddle), Jessie Cobb (Mandolin) Mike Anglin (bass) and Garnet Imes Bowman (harmony vocals).
