Phoebe Dynevor Biography

Phoebe Dynevor is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on April 17, 1995 in Manchester, England, United Kingdom. Phoebe Harriet Dynevor (born 17 April 1995) is an English actress, the daughter of actress Sally Dynevor. She began her career starring in the BBC drama series Waterloo Road (2009–2010), and later appeared on Prisoners’ Wives (2012–2013) and Dickensian (2015–2016). In 2017, Dynevor made her American debut with a recurring role in the TV Land comedy-drama series Younger, and later starred in the Sony Crackle crime comedy series, Snatch (2017–2018). In 2020 she began starring in the Netflix period drama series Bridgerton.
