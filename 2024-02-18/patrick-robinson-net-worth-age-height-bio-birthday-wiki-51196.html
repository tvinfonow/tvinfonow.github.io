Patrick Robinson Biography

Patrick Robinson is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on November 6, 1963 in London, London, United Kingdom. Patrick Robinson (born 6 November 1963) is an English actor, best known for playing the character of staff nurse and later charge nurse (in early years of the show) and in later years, Consultant Martin “Ash” Ashford in the long-running medical drama series Casualty on BBC One.

In late 2007, he played former slave Thomas Peters who went on to become a leader in Sierra Leone, in a stage adaptation of Rough Crossings by historian Simon Schama at the Liverpool Playhouse.

In March 2011, he was appearing as a German cavalry officer ‘Friedrich’ in the New London Theatre production of War Horse.

In 2013, Patrick participated in the eleventh series of Strictly Come Dancing. His dance partner was Anya Garnis, a new professional dancer that year. They were voted out in the semi-final. He played Gregory Brantner in Schooled for Murder, a two part episode of Midsomer Murders (2013, S15). Patrick returned to Casualty in 2016 as part of the anniversary celebrations. In September 2015, he joined series 5 of Sky 1’s Mount Pleasant as Policeman Cameron Miller, and returned for series 6 in September 2016. In March 2020 he played Marcus Ormansby, a novelist, in “The Trouble with Maggie Cole” an ITV drama with Dawn French.

After leaving the series, he appeared as CIA agent Leon Washington in the 2003 action film Belly of the Beast, alongside Steven Seagal.
