Andrew Upton is one of the most popular and richest Director who was born on February 1, 1966 in Australia, Australia. Husband of Cate Blanchett and writer who penned plays for the Sydney Theatre Company and Royal National Theatre.

Upton and Cate Blanchett met in 1996 on the set of a TV show, and were married on 29 December 1997. They have four children: biological sons Dashiell John (born 3 December 2001), Roman Robert (born 23 April 2004), Ignatius Martin (born 13 April 2008), and adopted daughter Edith Vivian Patricia, whose adoption was confirmed on 6 March 2015.

Upton and Blanchett formed a film production company, Dirty Films, whose projects include the short film Bangers (1999) and the feature film Little Fish (2006).
