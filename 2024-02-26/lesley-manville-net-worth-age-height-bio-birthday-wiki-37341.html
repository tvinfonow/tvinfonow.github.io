Lesley Ann Manville OBE (born 12 March 1956) is an English actress. She is known for her frequent collaborations with director Mike Leigh with her winning the London Film Critics Circle Award for British Actress of the Year for Leigh’s All or Nothing (2002) and Another Year (2010), and the National Board of Review Award for Best Actress for the latter film. Her other collaborations with Leigh include Grown-Ups (1980), High Hopes (1988), Secrets & Lies (1996), Topsy-Turvy (1999), Vera Drake (2004) and Mr. Turner (2014). She was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for Phantom Thread (2017). Making her film debut in Dance with a Stranger (1985), her other film roles include High Season (1987), The Great Ecstasy of Robert Carmichael (2005), Sparkle (2007), A Christmas Carol (2009), Womb (2010), Spike Island (2012), The Christmas Candle (2013), Rupture (2016), Maleficent (2014), Molly Moon and the Incredible Book of Hypnotism (2015), Hampstead (2017), the Maleficent sequel Maleficent: Mistress of Evil (2019), Ordinary Love (2019) and Misbehaviour (2020).
