What is Jan Ernst matzeliger famous for?

Jan Ernst Matzeliger. Jan Matzeliger invented the automatic shoe lasting machine, mechanizing the complex process of joining a shoe sole to its upper, and revolutionizing the shoe industry. Matzeliger was born in Dutch Guiana (now Suriname) and was self-educated.

Why did Jan matzeliger invent shoes?

Matzeliger set out to find a solution to the problems he discerned in the shoemaking process. He thought there had to be a way to develop an automatic method for lasting shoes. He began coming up with designs for machines that could do the job.
