Monisha Kaltenborn is one of the most popular and richest Lawyer who was born on May 10, 1971 in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. Monisha Kaltenborn, nee Narang, was born 10 May 1971. She held a 33.3% share in the Sauber Formula One team until Longbow Finance S.A. took over in July 2016. From January 2010 to 22 June 2017, she was the chief executive officer of the team. She was Formula One’s first female team principal.

Kaltenborn returned to motorsport in February 2018 with the creation of KDC Racing. The Sauber F1 Team announced that Kaltenborn would be leaving Sauber Group as of 22 June 2017. This was via a statement posted on their website.
