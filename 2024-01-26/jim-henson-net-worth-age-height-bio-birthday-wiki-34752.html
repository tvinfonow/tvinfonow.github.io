What happened Jim Henson?

On May 16, 1990, Henson died in New York City at age 53 from streptococcal toxic shock syndrome caused by Streptococcus pyogenes. He posthumously received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 1991, and was named a Disney Legend in 2011.

Who does Jim Henson voice?

During his 34-year career as a puppeteer, Jim Henson created more than 400 different Muppets and provided the voices for Kermit the Frog, Ernie (of Bert and Ernie fame), TV host Guy Smiley, Rowlf the Dog, the Swedish Chef and the lovable curmudgeon, Waldorf.
