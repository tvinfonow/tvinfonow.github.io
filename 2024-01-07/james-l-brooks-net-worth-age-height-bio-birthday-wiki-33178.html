James L. Brooks Biography

James L. Brooks is one of the most popular and richest TV Producer who was born on May 9, 1940 in Brooklyn, New York State, United States. Known for his work on Terms of Endearment and The Simpsons. He founded the TV and film company Gracie Films.

He hired cartoonist Matt Groening to brainstorm some shorts while he was a producer on The Tracey Ullman Show in 1987. The resulting shorts featured a family known as The Simpsons.

He married Marianne Catherine Morrissey in 1964 and later, Holly Beth Holmberg in 1978.
