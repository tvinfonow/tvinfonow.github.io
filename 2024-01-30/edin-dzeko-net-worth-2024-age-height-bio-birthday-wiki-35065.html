Edin Dzeko Biography

Edin Dzeko is one of the most popular and richest Soccer Player who was born on March 17, 1986 in Sarajevo, Bosnia And Herzegovina. Bosnian striker who joined the Premier League’s Manchester City in 2011 after spending four seasons with VfL Wolfsburg. He became a member of the Bosnia and Herzegovina national team in 2007.

He and fellow striker Mario Balotelli played together on Manchester City from 2011 to 2013.
