Lars Lokke Rasmussen Biography

Lars Lokke Rasmussen is one of the most popular and richest World Leader who was born on May 15, 1964 in Denmark, Denmark. From 2009 until 2011, this Venstre (Danish conservative-leaning liberal party) politician held office as Prime Minister of Denmark. Previously, he had served as Minister of Finance and Minister of the Interior and Health.

In 2011, Helle Thorning-Schmidt succeeded Rasmussen as Prime Minister of Denmark.
