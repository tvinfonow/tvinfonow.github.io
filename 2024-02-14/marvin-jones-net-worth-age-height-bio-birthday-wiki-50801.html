Marvin Jones Biography

Marvin Jones is one of the most popular and richest Basketball Player who was born on December 29, 1993 in Chicago, Chicago, United States. Marvin Jermaine Jones (born October 8, 1993) is an American professional basketball player for Promitheas Patras of the Greek Basket League and the EuroCup. Standing at 2.13 m (7’0″), he plays at the power forward and center positions. After playing four years of college basketball Jones entered the 2017 NBA draft, but he was not selected in the draft’s two rounds.

On January 28, 2020, Jones returned to Greece and officially signed with EuroCup club Promitheas Patras.

On June 15, 2018, he signed a two-year deal with Slovenian club Olimpija.
