Eli Tomac Biography

Eli Tomac is one of the most popular and richest Motorcycle Racer who was born on November 14, 1992 in Colorado, Colorado, United States. Pro racer who became the winner of the 2017 AMA Motocross Championship. He won his first 450MX race in 2014 and was also the 2013 250MX champion.

He joined Adam Cianciarulo on the Monster Energy Kawasaki team for 2016.

In 2010, Eli Tomac became the second rider in the history of the sport, behind James Stewart, to win his professional 250cc class AMA Pro Motocross debut at the Hangtown season opener in Rancho Cordova, California. At that time he was riding for Team Geico Honda.

Tomac began his professional career in 2010. He made his 450cc debut part way into the 2013 Supercross schedule, and moved to the class full-time the following year.

His father is John Tomac, a Mountain Bike Hall of Fame member.
