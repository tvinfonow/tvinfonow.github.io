Earl Tupper Biography

Earl Tupper is one of the most popular and richest Inventor who was born on July 28, 1907 in New Hampshire, New Hampshire, United States. Famous for inventing Tupperware plastic storage containers, he established the Tupperware Plastics Company and began selling his products in the late 1930s.

He and Teflon developer Roy Plunkett are both notable for their revolutionary plastics inventions.

He spent his early years in Berlin, New Hampshire, and later lived and worked in Rhode Island, Massachusetts, and Florida. After divorcing his wife in the late 1950s, he settled in Costa Rica.
