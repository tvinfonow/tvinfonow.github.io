Demetrius Cesar Andrade (/ˈ æ n d r ə d / AN -drəd; born February 26, 1988) is an American professional boxer. He has held multiple world championships in two weight classes, including the WBO middleweight title since 2018, and previously the WBA (Regular) and WBO light middleweight titles between 2013 and 2017. As an amateur he won the U.S. national championships and Golden Gloves twice each, a gold medal at the 2007 World Championships, and represented the U.S. at the 2008 Olympics; all in the welterweight division.

Demetrius Andrade obtained arguably the most successful amateur boxing reputation amongst all Rhode Island fighters in history.
