TaRanda Greene Biography

TaRanda Greene is one of the most popular and richest Musician who was born on April 6, 1979 in Saint Petersburg, Russia.

TaRanda Greene Beene (née, Kiser; born April 6, 1979) is an American Christian musician, who sings Southern gospel, worship and Christian Contemporary music. She has released seven studio albums, Each Day You Face (2003), A Thing Called Love (2006), In The Sanctuary (2008), Stronger (2014), Spirit of Christmas (2015), A Collection (2017), and The Healing (2018).

Although previously a member of her family group, her music career began with The Greenes in 1997, while her solo career started in 2003, with her first individually released album Everyday You Face. She released Stronger in 2014 with Stowtown Records, followed by “Spirit of Christmas” in 2015. In 2016, she began a musical endeavor with Cana’s Voice.
