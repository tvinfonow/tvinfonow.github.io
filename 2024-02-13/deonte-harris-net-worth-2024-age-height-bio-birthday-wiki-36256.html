Deonte Harris (born December 4, 1997) is an American football wide receiver and return specialist for the New Orleans Saints of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Assumption College in Worcester, Massachusetts, and signed with the Saints as a rookie undrafted free agent in 2019 where he made his first Pro Bowl as a returner. Harris grew up in the Baltimore suburb of Middle River, Maryland and played high school football at Archbishop Curley High School where he also lettered in basketball and track & field.
