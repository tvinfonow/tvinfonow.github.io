How long were Ashford and Simpson married?

For almost 40 years, Nick Ashford and Valerie Simpson—partners in music and marriage—made up the songwriting duo Ashford & Simpson. They spent nearly 33 of those years in the same New York City townhome until Nick lost his battle with throat cancer in August 2011.

How did Nick Ashford and Valerie Simpson meet?

Ashford and Simpson met in 1964 in a New York City church. Ashford, who was born in 1941 in Fairfield, S.C., and raised in Willow Run, Mich., had come to the city to pursue a dance career. Simpson was a music student, and after they connected, they decided to write songs together.

Where is Valerie Simpson now?
