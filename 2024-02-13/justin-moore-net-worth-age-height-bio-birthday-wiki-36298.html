Justin Cole Moore (born March 30, 1984) is an American country music singer and songwriter, signed to Big Machine Records imprint Valory Music Group. For that label, he has released five studio albums: his self titled debut in 2009, Outlaws Like Me in 2011, Off the Beaten Path in 2013, Kinda Don’t Care in 2016, and Late Nights and Longnecks in 2019. He has also charted sixteen times on the US Billboard Hot Country Songs and Country Airplay charts, including with the number 1 singles “Small Town USA”, “If Heaven Wasn’t So Far Away”, “Til My Last Day”, “Lettin’ the Night Roll”, “You Look Like I Need a Drink”, “Somebody Else Will”, and “The Ones That Didn’t Make It Back Home”; and the top 10 hits “Backwoods” and “Point at You”.
