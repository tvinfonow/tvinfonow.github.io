Hayley Jones Biography

Hayley Jones is one of the most popular and richest Sport Cyclist who was born on September 26, 1995 in Neath, United Kingdom. Hayley Jones is the child of Phil Jones and Louise Jones. She was the sprint gold medalist for Wales in the 1990 Commonwealth Games.

Jones participated in the UCI Juniors Track World Championships on 8 August 2013 at the Sir Chris Hoy Velodrome, Glasgow. She was part the Team Pursuit team from Britain, which included Emily Kay, Amy Hill and Emily Nelson. They broke the world record in the qualifying heat. This was only a few weeks after it had been set at the European Track Championships. The new time was 4:38.708. They beat Russia in the final to break the record again with a time 4:35.085. The 20-kilometre (12 mile) Points Race was her next race, where she won Bronze.

Jones was part British Cycling’s Olympic Development Programme 2012/2013.
