Izabella St. James is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on September 25, 1975 in Kraków, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Poland. Izabella St. James (born Izabella Katarina Kasprzyk on 25 September 1975) is a writer, actress and a former girlfriend of Hugh M. Hefner, editor/publisher of Playboy magazine. She is best known as the author of a memoir entitled Bunny Tales: Behind Closed Doors at the Playboy Mansion published August 21, 2006, about activities at the famed Playboy Mansion in Los Angeles.

In 2015 she starred in the first season of the Polish reality TV series called Żony Hollywood (English Hollywood Wives) which is based on The Real Housewives franchise.

Since leaving the Playboy Mansion, St. James has been acting and writing. In August 2006 she published Bunny Tales: Behind Closed Doors at the Playboy Mansion, a memoir of her time as one of Hugh Hefner’s girlfriends. St. James lives in Los Angeles.
