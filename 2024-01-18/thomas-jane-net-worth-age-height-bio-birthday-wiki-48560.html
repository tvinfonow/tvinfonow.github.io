Thomas Jane (born Thomas Elliott III; February 22, 1969) is an American actor. He is the founder of RAW Studios, an entertainment company he uses to release comic books he has written, the first of which was Bad Planet. He made his directorial debut with the crime thriller Dark Country (2009), in which he also starred.
