Steve McKinney is one of the most popular and richest American Football Player who was born on October 15, 1975 in Galveston, Texas, United States. Stephen Michael McKinney (born October 15th 1975) was a former offensive guard for the National Football League. He was selected to the Indianapolis Colts in the fourth round of the 1998 NFL Draft. He played in the NCAA football tournament during his time at Texas A&M.

He quit the Colts as an unrestricted free agent in the 2002 NFL Season. He was the very first unrestricted free agent accepted with the Houston Texans. He played every single game at center and took over every offensive move. In 2003, he began every single game at center , for the 2nd time in a row. season. He also joined Chester Pitts as the only two Texans offensive linemen who started every game of the short history of the club. He was then the first to start all 32 games during the seasons of 2004 and 2005 with the Texans.

McKinney also operated Velocity Sports Performance training centers in the Houston area from 2004-2008. In 2007 McKinney began his own hunting and whitetail deer breeding operation called McKinney Whitetails, in Marquez, TX near his hometown of Centerville, TX.

On September 23, 2007, McKinney suffered a torn ACL in a loss to the Colts. He was placed on injured reserve, ending his season. He was released the following offseason.
