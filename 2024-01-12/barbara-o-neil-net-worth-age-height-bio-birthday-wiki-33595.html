Barbara O'Neil Biography

Barbara O'Neil is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on July 17, 1910 in Missouri, Missouri, United States. Remembered for her role as Ellen O’Hara in the classic 1939 film Gone with the Wind, this screen actress went on to earn an Oscar nomination for her performance in the 1940 movie All This, and Heaven Too.

She played the role of Theresa Randolph in the Otto Preminger-directed 1949 movie Whirlpool.

During the early 1940s, she was briefly married to writer and director Joshua Logan.
