Top Facts about Elizabeth Arden

  • Elizabeth Arden was a Canadian-American businesswoman and entrepreneur.
  • She founded the cosmetics company Elizabeth Arden, Inc. in 1910.
  • Arden was one of the first women to enter the male-dominated field of cosmetics.
  • Her company introduced many innovative products, including the first travel-sized beauty products.
  • By 1929, she owned salons in over 100 cities worldwide.
  • During World War II, her company produced cosmetics for the U.S. military.
  • Arden was known for her philanthropy and support of women’s causes.
  • She died in 1966 at age 87 with an estimated net worth of $60 million.
  • The Elizabeth Arden brand is still popular today and offers a wide range of skincare and makeup products.
