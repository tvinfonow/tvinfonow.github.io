Bridget Regan Biography

Bridget Regan is one of the most popular and richest TV Actress who was born on February 3, 1982 in San Diego, California, United States. Actress who is best known for her role as Kahlan in the TV show Legend of the Seeker. She also played Rebecca Lowe on the series White Collar.

She was in the show NCIS: Los Angeles with She appeared on an episode of NCIS: Los Angeles with LL Cool J.

Since 2006, Regan has appeared in various television shows and in films. In 2008, she began filming Legend of the Seeker, a television show based on Terry Goodkind’s Sword of Truth series. She portrayed Mother Confessor Kahlan Amnell, which earned her a cult following. Although her natural hair is red and curly, Regan dyed it dark brown and black, and occasionally chemically straightened it, for Legend of the Seeker. In 2009, she ventured into producing with Camp Wanatachi, a musical that ran in New York at La MaMa Experimental Theatre Club.

Regan is popular with the comic book community. From 2009 to 2013, she was commonly selected among comic book fans and critics as their ideal choice to portray Wonder Woman. In 2009, Rick Marshall of MTV stated, “In one of the most voted-on, yet lop-sided weekly polls we’ve ever conducted here on Splash Page, Legend of the Seeker actress Bridget Regan was voted fans’ favored lady to bring DC’s iconic Amazon warrior, Wonder Woman, to the big screen.” Regan netted over 62 percent of the total vote, well ahead of well-known and lesser-known actresses. In 2013, Screen Rant’s Andrew Dyce commented, “Casting Wonder Woman may sound like a tall order, but if you ask the online fan communities, Bridget Regan may be the name you hear more than any other.” In 2014, Regan was cast in the recurring role of Dottie for 2015 in the comic book-based television series Agent Carter.

She was married to Eamon O’Sullivan in the year 2010. The couple O’Sullivan welcomed their daughter Frankie Jean in 2010 and an infant baby boy with the name of Bernard Moon in 2018.
