Karen Fukuhara Biography

Karen Fukuhara is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on February 10, 1992 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Actress best known for her role as Katana in the 2016 DC Comics film Suicide Squad. She also starred in the 2017 film Stray.

She was cast to star alongside Margot Robbie, Will Smith, and Joel Kinnaman in Suicide Squad.

Fukuhara attended the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) while working as a reporter for a sports show on NHK in Japan. She was a member of the a cappella group, Medleys, whose alumni include fellow actress Kelly Marie Tran. Fukuhara graduated from UCLA in 2014 with a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology and a minor in Theater.

In early December 2016, Fukuhara announced that she had completed the first week of filming for Stray (alongside Christine Woods, Miyavi, and Ross Partridge).

She was born and raised in both the United States and Japan.
