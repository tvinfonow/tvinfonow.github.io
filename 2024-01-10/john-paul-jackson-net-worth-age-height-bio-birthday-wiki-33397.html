Jackson often focused on supernatural topics like dreams, visions, and dream interpretation as found in the Bible. He developed a number of prophetic training courses. He was the founder of Streams Ministries International, a Christian group that deals especially with the practice known as prophecy. Jackson’s career spanned more than 20 years.
