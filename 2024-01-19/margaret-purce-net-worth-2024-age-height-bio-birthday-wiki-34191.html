Margaret Purce Biography

Margaret Purce is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on September 18, 1995 in Silver Spring, Maryland, United States. Margaret “Midge” Purce, born September 18, 1995, is an American soccer player. She plays forward for Sky Blue FC in National Women’s Soccer League. She played previously with Portland Thorns FC from 2018-2019, Boston Breakers 2017 and Harvard Crimson 2013-2016.

Purce was called up to the United States women’s national team in 2017 Tournament of Nations, but she didn’t play in any of three games. In June 2018, Purce was called up for two friendlies against China PR. However, she suffered an ankle injury during training camp and was therefore unable to play in the friendlies.

Purce made 21 appearances and 16 starts for the Thorns in 2018, playing primarily as a right fullback. During the 2019 NWSL season, Purce moved into wide and central forward positions, and at one point scored 5 goals in a span of 5 games.

After the Breakers folded in January 2018, the league held a dispersal draft for the Breakers’ players; Purce was selected with the 4th pick by the Portland Thorns.

Purce was a Harvard University women’s soccer player who scored 42 goals in 69 games. She was also named Ivy League Player-of-the-Year twice in 2015 and 2016 and a first-team NSCAA All-American for 2016. Purce studied psychology at Harvard to pursue a career in law.
