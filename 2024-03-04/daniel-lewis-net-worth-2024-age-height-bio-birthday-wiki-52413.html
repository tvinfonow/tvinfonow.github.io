Daniel Lewis is one of the most popular and richest Boxer who was born on December 18, 1993 in Penrith, New South Wales, Australia. Daniel Lewis (born 18 December 1993) is an Australian professional boxer. While a student, Daniel Lewis earned the gold medal for Australia during the Commonwealth Youth Games.
