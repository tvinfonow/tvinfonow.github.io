Robert Matthew “Matt” Barrie (born 16 August 1973) is an Australian tech businessman. The chief executive director of Freelancer.com which is a global online marketplace for crowdsourcing and freelancing. He is an adjunct Professor of The University of Sydney where he gives classes on computer and security for networks since 2001. technology venture creation since.

The year 2006 was the one when Barrie was awarded his first New South Wales Pearcey Award for his contributions towards his contribution to the IT&T industry. He is a renowned alumnus from his alma mater, the University of Sydney, having been awarded the Alumnus of the Year Award from The Faculty of Engineering and IT in 2010.

In 2011, Barrie was named in the ten most influential people in Australian IT by the International Business Times. Barrie has a Masters in Applied Finance at Macquarie University and a graduate certificate of applied finance at FINSIA. He also graduated from the EPGC (2005) and the Stanford Executive Program (2011) from the Stanford Graduate School of Business. He is also a Fellow of the Institute of Engineers Australia (FIEAust).
