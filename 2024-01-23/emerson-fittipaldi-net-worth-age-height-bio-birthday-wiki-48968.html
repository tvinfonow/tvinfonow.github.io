Top Facts about Emerson Fittipaldi

  • Two-time Formula One World Champion (1972, 1974).
  • First Brazilian to win the Indy 500 (1989).
  • Founded Fittipaldi Automotive team in 1974.
  • Won 14 Grand Prix races during his F1 career.
  • Inducted into International Motorsports Hall of Fame in 2001.
  • Retired from racing at age 50 in 1996.
  • Started racing go-karts at age 14.
  • Won the prestigious Daytona 24 Hours race twice (1980,1985).
  • Served as president of the Brazilian Racing Drivers Association.
  • Has two sons who also became professional racecar drivers
