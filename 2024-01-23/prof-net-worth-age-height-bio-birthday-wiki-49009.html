Prof Biography

Prof is one of the most popular and richest Rapper who was born on April 29, 1984 in Minneapolis, United States. Jacob Anderson, better known as Prof, was born April 29, 1984. He is an American rapper and singer. He is based in Minneapolis. In 2007, he released Project Gampo, his first full-length album. He has since released three more albums and three EPs. City Pages listed Prof among the top 20 Minnesota rappers in 2012 and named him as their co-owner. Prof, who was co-owner of Stophouse Music Group in 2012, signed to Rhymesayers Entertainment late 2013.

Stophouse Music Group released all albums until 2013. Rhymesayers Entertainment released “Liability”, Pookie Baby, and “POWDERHORN SUITES”.

In May 2013, when Busta Rhymes failed to show up at the Soundset Music Festival in Shakopee, Minnesota, Prof substituted for him with only an hour’s notice.

In a May 2015, Prof announced his debut record on Rhymesayers, entitled Liability. In the same interview, he also revealed he had signed with William Morris Endeavor for his future touring ventures. Prof released a single called “Ghost” from the album. Liability was released October 16, 2015, and peaked at 141 on the Billboard 200 chart November 7, 2015.

City Pages voted Prof the 19th-best Minnesota rapper in 2012. They cited his musical prowess, his “impressive singing voice”, and his ability engage and make people laugh. Dwight Hobbes, Twin Cities Daily Planet musician and critic, said that Prof’s 2011 album King Gampo was “asinine and narcissistic self indulgence run completely riot without a single shred of redeeming artifacts” and “rap as it is at its worst”.
