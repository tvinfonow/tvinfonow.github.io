Gwendolyn Osborne-Smith (born 7 August 1978 in Bath, Somerset, England) is a model and actress who spent 12 years on The Price Is Right as a model. Born to a Jamaican mother and British father, Osborne married retired NBA player (and current TNT basketball analyst) Kenny Smith on 2 September 2006. They have two children together (Malloy and London) and Smith is stepfather to Osborne’s daughter (Monique) from a previous relationship.
