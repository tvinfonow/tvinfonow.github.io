In 2010, Mandana left Paul Hastings to help launch the Rachel Zoe Collection, a contemporary womenswear collection, and to define and grow the company’s initiatives in business development, digital media, strategic investments, licensing, publishing, brand partnerships, endorsements, and television production. Mandana held this position until 2015 when she left the company.

Most recently, Mandana was the Chief Brand Officer of Everything But The House, an online marketplace for estate sales which she joined in 2015 to lead its rebranding and build teams dedicated to developing and optimizing its user experience, building one cohesive cross-channel brand, expanding business development and generating global awareness. She oversees the company’s brand strategy, marketing, partnerships, merchandising, new launches, and global communications. Mandana serves as part of the leadership team which has raised $84.5 million in venture capital and is the Executive Producer of their TV project for HGTV.
