Brian Mast is one of the most popular and richest Politician who was born on July 10, 1980 in Grand Rapids, Michigan, United States. Brian Jeffery Mast (born July 10 in 1988) is an American politician and veteran, who is representative for the U.S. representative for Florida’s 18th congressional district. Mast is a member of the Republican Party.

After being discharged with honor of the U.S. Army, Mast was appointed as an explosives expert for the United States Department of Homeland Security. As he recovered from injury from his injuries at Walter Reed Army Medical Center, Mast provided explosive and counter-terrorism advice for members of the Office of Emergency Operations at the National Nuclear Security Administration from July 2011 through February 2012 as well as in the capacity of an instructor for Home Made explosives for the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives.

In January 2015, Mast volunteered with the Israel Defense Forces, working at a base outside Tel Aviv, packing medical kits and moving supplies.

During 2015–2016, Mast accepted $4,950 USD in campaign donations from the NRA’s Political Victory Fund.

Mast received an invitation from President Barack Obama as a guest to his State of the Union address and was in the same room as the First Lady Michelle Obama and Second Lady Jill Biden. Mast was selected as one of the 10 House freshman to watch by The Hill, a Washington, D.C. newspaper The Hill, out of 55 freshmen House members.
