Top Facts about Brandi Glanville

  • Brandi Glanville is an American television personality, former model, and author.

  • She has also appeared on several reality shows including Celebrity Apprentice, The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip, Celebrity Big Brother, Famously Single, and My Kitchen Rules.
  • Glanville has launched a clothing line called “Brand B” and has a weekly podcast show titled Brandi Glanville Unfiltered.
  • She has released her own Chardonnay titled “Unfiltered Blonde” and an Android application called Blunder Block.
  • Glanville has a son with actor Eddie Cibrian whom she divorced in 2010 after he had an affair with country singer LeAnn Rimes.
  • Glanville was sued by fellow RHOBH cast member, Joanna Krupa in 2015 for defamation for her comments made on Watch What Happens Live and settled for $500,000 in 2017.
  • Glanville served as the Celebrity Grand Marshal at the Sacramento Pride in 2013.
