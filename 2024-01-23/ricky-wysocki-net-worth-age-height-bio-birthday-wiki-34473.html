Ricky Wysocki Net Worth

Ricky Wysocki is one of the richest Disc Golfer from United States. According to our analysis, Wikipedia, Forbes & Business Insider, Ricky Wysocki's net worth $5 Million. (Last Update: December 11, 2023)

Wysocki became a professional in 2010. As of May 2019, his career earnings total $366,409.76. Wysocki, a two-time PDGA – World Champion, is widely regarded to be one of the most talented players in the world. He currently holds the second highest PDGA rating, after Paul McBeth.

Wysocki was the first male player to win the PDGA Male Player of the Year Award and the PDGA Rookie of the Year Award. Wysocki defeated 4x World Champion Paul McBeth twice to win the PDGA World Championship. He also won the PDGA National Tour Series in 2016, the Disc Golf World Tour points series and the Disc Golf Pro Tour point series in 2016.
