Paul Mullin Biography

Paul Mullin is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on November 6, 1994 in Litherland, Litherland, United Kingdom. Paul Philip Mullin (born 6 November 1994) is an English professional footballer who plays as a forward for Cambridge United of League Two on loan from League One club Tranmere Rovers.

In June 2018, he signed for Tranmere Rovers. He scored his first goal for Tranmere in a 4-3 EFL Trophy loss against Crewe Alexandra on 4 September 2018.

In January 2020, Tranmere Rovers took Premier League Watford FC to an FA Cup 3rd round replay after drawing 3–3 at Vicarage Road, Mullin scored a penalty in the first game and went on to score the winner after coming on as a substitute in the replay at Prenton Park. Tranmere drew Manchester United in the 4th round at home.

On 27 June 2017, Mullin joined League two club Swindon Town for an undisclosed fee. .
