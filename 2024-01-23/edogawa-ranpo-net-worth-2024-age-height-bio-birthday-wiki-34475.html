Edogawa Ranpo Biography

Edogawa Ranpo is one of the most popular and richest Novelist who was born on October 21, 1894 in Japan, Japan. Best known for his mystery novels and stories featuring the detective character of Kogoro Akechi, this popular Japanese writer published such individual titles as The Human Leopard, The Conjurer, and The Fruits of Curiosity-Hunting.

He was greatly influenced by the work of Edgar Allan Poe and based his pen name (Edogawa Ranpo) on Poe’s name.

Born Tarō Hirai in Japan’s Mie Prefecture, he was the grandson of a samurai warrior.
