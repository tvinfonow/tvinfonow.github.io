James Pankow Biography

James Pankow is one of the most popular and richest Rock Singer who was born on August 20, 1947 in St. Louis, Missouri, United States. Best known for his work with the popular band Chicago, this songwriter, musical arranger, and trombonist also performed with Three Dog Night, the Bee Gees, and Toto.

He and Eagles guitarist Don Felder had a close friendship.

With his first wife, Karen Marie Green, he had a daughter named Sarah and a son named Jonathan. His second marriage, to Jeanne Elisabeth Pacelli, resulted in a daughter named Lillian and a son named Carter.
