Susan Olsen Biography

Susan Olsen is one of the most popular and richest TV Actress who was born on August 14, 1961 in Santa Monica, California, United States. A former actor as a child who played Cindy Brady on the television show The Brady Bunch and the spin-off show The Bradys.

She was Florence Henderson’s on-screen daughter in The Brady Bunch.

As a teen, Olsen was the spokesgirl for Sindy doll, made by Marx Toys from the mid-1960s. As an adult, Olsen moved into the graphic design business and in 1998 briefly marketed a brand of glow-in-the-dark shoes for Converse. She also worked as a talk show host at the Los Angeles radio station KLSX from 1995 to 1996 with Ken Ober, and co-hosted and co-wrote another radio show with comedian Allan Havey at Comedy World in 2000.

Susan Marie Olsen (born August 14, 1961) is an American actress, singer, voice actress, animal welfare advocate, artist, and former radio host. Olsen is known for her role as Cindy Brady, the youngest Brady child in the sitcom The Brady Bunch for the full run of the show, from 1969 to 1974.

She was engaged with Steve Ventimiglia from 1988 to 1990, as well as Mitch Markwell from 1995 to 2004.
