Doris Day Biography

Doris Day is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on April 3, 1922 in Cincinnati, Ohio, United States. Singer and actress who appeared in romantic comedies with Rock Hudson. One of her first hits was “Sentimental Journey,” which was released in 1945.

President George W. Bush awarded her the Presidential Medal of Freedom for her work on behalf of animal welfare.

She married Al Jorden in March 1941, then George Weidler on March 30, 1946, then Martin Melcher on April 3, 1951, and finally Barry Comden on April 14, 1976. She and Melcher had a son named Terry, who was a musician and record producer.
