Killah Priest Biography

Killah Priest is one of the most popular and richest Rapper who was born on August 16, 1970 in Brooklyn, New York State, United States. Best known for his association with the Wu-Tang Clan, this New York-based rapper also performed with a hip hop supergroup called The HRSMN. Known for such solo albums as Heavy Mental and View from Masada, he frequently included controversial and highly political lyrics in his songs.

He and fellow New York rapper Ghostface Killah both joined the Wu-Tang Clan.

He was born Walter Reed in New York City’s Brooklyn neighborhood.
