Dillard played catcher at Saltillo High School in Saltillo, Mississippi. In the 2002 draft, he was selected again by the Brewers, this time in the 34th round as the 1,009th overall pick. Dillard signed with the Brewers on May 27, 2003. Rather than continue his catching career, the Brewers informed him that he would be utilized as a pitcher, a position he had only occasionally played in high school.
