William Saliba Biography

William Saliba is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on March 24, 2001 in Bondy, Île-de-France, France. William Alain André Gabriel Saliba (born 24 March 2001) is a French professional footballer who plays as a defender for Ligue 1 club Saint-Étienne on loan from Premier League club Arsenal.

On 25 July 2019, Arsenal announced that Saliba had signed a “long-term” contract with the club, and would join up with the team after staying on loan at Saint-Étienne for the 2019–20 season. Media reported a contract duration of five years and that the transfer fee paid to Saint-Étienne amounted to £27 million. Arsenal faced competition from rivals Tottenham Hotspur to complete the deal, with both clubs meeting Saint-Étienne’s valuation of the player, however, Saliba chose to join Arsenal, with the club’s interest in Saliba dating back to autumn of 2018.
