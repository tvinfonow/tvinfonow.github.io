3. He was awarded the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award for 2018 for Sugam Sangeet.
4. In 2020, the Government of India honoured him with the Padma Shri.
5. He won the Madan Mohan Best Male Playback Singer Award at the Sur-Singar competition in 1976.
6. He is also a recipient of the 2004 Lata Mangeshkar Puraskar instituted by Madhya Pradesh government.
7. He got awarded by Late Sadashiv Amarapurkar Award 2017 by Ahmednagar’s Think Global Foundation.

13. He directs the Suresh Wadkar Ajivasan Music Academy in Mumbai.
