Nicole Parker Biography

Nicole Parker is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on February 21, 1978 in Irvine, United States. Nicole Frances Parker (born February 21 1977) was an American comedian, actress writer, singer, and actress. She is most famous for her role in Fox’s comedy sketch series Mad TV (2003-2009, 2016). She appeared on the show in June 2009 and wrapped up her role in the role of Elphaba on The Broadway show Wicked the Musical, a role she reprised in a tour of North America. In 2017, Parker has voiced Penelope Pitstop in the Wacky Races animated series. Parker has also been in comedy film Meet the Spartans (2008) and Disaster Movie (2008).

As of 2009, Parker was replaced by Marcie Dodd as Elphaba as part of her role in the Broadway production of musical Wicked beginning on January 16 to 16. She was in the show with Alli Mauzey in the role of Glinda. Her departure was announced on 19 July after the expiration of her contract of six months, being replaced with Dee Roscioli.

Parker again starred as Elphaba in Wicked on the show’s first North American tour. She reunited with Mauzey beginning performances in Denver, Colorado May 18, 2012. She replaced Mamie Parris. She later left the production on September 24, 2012.

From July 2006 until January 2007 Parker was in the musical comedy Martin Short: Fame Becomes Me.
