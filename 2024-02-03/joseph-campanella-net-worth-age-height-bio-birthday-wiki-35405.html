Top Facts about Joseph Campanella

  • Joseph Anthony Campanella was an American character actor who appeared in over 200 television and film roles from the early 1950s to 2009.
  • He was born in Manhattan, New York City to Sicilian immigrants Philip and Mary O. Campanella.
  • Campanella served in the United States Navy during World War II, at 18 one of the youngest commanders in the Navy.
  • He was nominated for a Daytime and Primetime Emmy Award and a Tony Award throughout his career.
  • He voiced the character of Dr. Curt Connors/The Lizard on Spider-Man: The Animated Series (1994–1997).
  • He hosted the Canadian educational program Science International between 1976 and 1979.
  • He had a recurring role as Joe Turino on the long-running CBS daytime drama Guiding Light.
  • He had a recurring role as Lew Wickersham in season 1 (1967–1968) of the television series Mannix.
  • One of his most popular roles was as Brian Darrell from 1969 to 1972 in The Bold Ones: The Lawyers.
  • Campanella had a recurring role as Harper Deveraux on the soap opera Days of Our Lives from 1987 to 1988.
  • He played Ann Romano’s (Bonnie Franklin) ex-husband, Ed Cooper, in eight episodes of One Day at a Time (1975–1984).
  • Campanella provided the voice-over for BMW commercials in the United States.
  • For several years beginning in the 1970s, He was a spokesman for NAPA Auto Parts.
  • He narrated the Discover science series on the Disney Channel from 1992 to 1994.
  • He died at the age of 93 on May 16, 2018, at his home in Sherman Oaks, California of complications from Parkinson’s disease.
