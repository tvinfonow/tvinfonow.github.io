Kevin VanDam Biography

Kevin VanDam is one of the most popular and richest Athlete who was born on October 14, 1967 in Kalamazoo, United States. Kevin VanDam (born October 14, 1967), often called simply “KVD,” is a professional bass fisherman from Otsego, Michigan. He is the all-time money winner in professional bass fishing, having earned $6,261,476.33 through September 2017. He has captured four Bassmaster Classic titles (2001, 2005, 2010, 2011), seven Toyota Tundra Angler of Year titles (1992, ‘96, ‘99, ‘08, ‘09, ’10, “11) and was also the FLW Angler of the Year in 2001. VanDam has predominantly fished the Bass Anglers Sportsman Society (BASS) Bassmasters tournament series during his career but was also a founding member of Major League Fishing.
