Did Archie Bell serve in Vietnam?

Archie Bell was drafted into the United States Army on May 12, 1967, and stationed in Vietnam. While on “rest and recuperation” leave from the Army, Bell recorded “Tighten Up,” which he and Billy Butler wrote with musical accompaniment by the Texas Southern University Tornadoes.
