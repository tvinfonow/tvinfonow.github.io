Olivia Moultrie Biography

Olivia Moultrie is one of the most popular and richest Soccer Player who was born on September 17, 2005 in California, United States. The soccer star made headlines when she became the first female athlete to ever play at the U.S. Soccer Development Academy to compete in full-time competition against males. She was a member of the Southern California teams SoCal Blues SC and Total Futbol Academy. She was awarded the CONCACAF U15 gold medal with the US Soccer Youth National Team.

She as well as She and Lorenzo Greer were both considered young soccer prodigies. were both considered soccer players of the future.

In February 2019 she announced that she was turning professional, having signed a representation deal with the Wasserman Media Group. She has signed with Nike to a multiyear endorsement arrangement.

Moultrie is from Canyon Country, California and started training in soccer when she was 4 years old. By fifth grade she was home schooled so that she could focus on soccer and she became the first girl on a boy’s club team to play in the United States Development Academy system. She has trained in Europe with clubs including Bayern Munich, Olympique Lyon and Paris Saint-Germain. At 11 she accepted a full scholarship to play soccer for The University of North Carolina when she reached college age. She was the youngest girls’ soccer player to publicly accept a college offer at the time. She is now the youngest girls’ soccer player to pass up her college eligibility in favor of turning pro.

The parents of her, KC and Jessica Moultrie were both former athletes.
