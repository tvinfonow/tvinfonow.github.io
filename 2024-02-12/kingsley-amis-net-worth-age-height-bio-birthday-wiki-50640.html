Kingsley Amis Biography

Kingsley Amis is one of the most popular and richest Novelist who was born on April 16, 1922 in London, England, United Kingdom. A twentieth-century British writer, poet, educator, and literary critic, he is best remembered for his humorous novels. His most famous works include Lucky Jim and That Uncertain Feeling.

He and W.H. Auden served as editors of different editions of the same British poetry anthology.

He had three children, Margin, Sally and Philip, with his first wife, Hilary Bardwell, before leaving her for his second wife, Elizabeth Jane Howard.
