Neil Diamond Biography

Neil Diamond is one of the most popular and richest Rock Singer who was born on January 24, 1941 in Brooklyn, New York State, United States. He is one of the most popular adult contemporary artists in Billboard history and has sold more than 125 million records. His hits include “Kentucky Woman”, “Sweet Caroline” and “America.”

He and Barbra Strreisand were Erasmus Hall High School classmates. They would sing a duet to Diamond’s “You Don’t Bring Me Flowers”, which would go on to become a #1 hit.

Jaye Posner was his first wife in 1963. Marcia Murphey married him on December 5, 1969. Katie McNeil followed on April 21, 2012. He is the father of Micah and Jesse and Marjorie, as well as two daughters named Elyn and Marjorie.
