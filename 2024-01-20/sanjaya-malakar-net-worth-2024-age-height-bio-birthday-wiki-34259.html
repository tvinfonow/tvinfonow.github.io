What is the meaning of Sanjaya?

Sanjaya or sanjay (Sanskrit: संजय, meaning “victory”) or Sanjaya Gavalgana is a character from the ancient Indian Hindu war epic Mahābhārata. In Mahabharata—An ancient story of a war between the Pandavas and the Kauravas—the blind king Dhritarashtra is the father of the principals of the Kaurava side.
