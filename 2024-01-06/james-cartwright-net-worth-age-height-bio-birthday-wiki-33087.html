James Cartwright Biography

James Cartwright is one of the most popular and richest War Hero who was born on September 22, 1949 in Rockford, Illinois, United States. The recipient of four Defense Distinguished Service Medals, this United States Marine Corps general served from 2007 until 2011 as Vice Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. From 2004 until 2007, he held the title of Commander of U.S. Strategic Command.

When Cartwright was investigated by the U.S. Justice Department in regard to a potential classified information leak, his lawyer, one-time White House attorney Greg Craig, refused to comment on the situation.

Cartwright, nicknamed “Hoss,” was born and raised in Rockford, Illinois.
