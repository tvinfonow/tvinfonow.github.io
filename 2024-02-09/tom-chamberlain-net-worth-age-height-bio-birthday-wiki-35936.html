Who was Thomas Chamberlain?

Thomas Davee Chamberlain (29 April 1841 – 12 August 1896) was a Lieutenant-Colonel of the US Army during the American Civil War. He was the brother of Joshua Chamberlain, his commander as the leader of the 20th Maine Volunteer Infantry Regiment.

What did Joshua Chamberlain do in the Battle of Gettysburg?

At the Battle of Gettysburg on July 2, 1863, holding the far left of the Union line and low on ammunition, Chamberlain ordered the 20th Maine forward in a bayonet charge at Little Round Top, dislodging and capturing over 100 Confederate troops and securing the tenuous Union position.
