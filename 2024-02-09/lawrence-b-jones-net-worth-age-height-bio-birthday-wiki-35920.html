Lawrence B. Jones Biography

Lawrence B. Jones is one of the most popular and richest Radio Personality who was born on December 10, 1992 in Texas, United States. Lawrence Billy Jones III (born on the 10th of December in 1992) is a African American conservative commentator, Fox News contributor, talk radio host and author. Jones became famous in 2015 after he was able to raise funds for a pizza restaurant that was unable to host an event for gay couples.

Jones was employed as a student advocate for Jones was appointed as a student advocate in the Garland Independent School District, becoming the youngest employee. At the age of 19 years old, Jones ran for a seat in the board of school in the district, but lost the race. He was an official in Dallas County Child Welfare. The year 2016 was the last time Jones joined the Garland Parks and Recreation Board.

In February 2018, Jones became editor-in-chief of the conservative website Campus Reform.

In July 2019, Jones blamed the Barack Obama Administration for a lenient plea deal that sex offender and accused child sex trafficker Jeffrey Epstein received in 2008, falsely claiming that Obama was president at the time.

In April of 2015, Jones launched a campaign to raise money in support of Memories Pizza, a pizza restaurant located in Indiana that was closed following negative feedback from the owners who stated that they’d not cater for gay weddings when requested. The event raised $844,000 for the family. The money was used for costs and a portion of it was donated to charities and to the church of the owner.
