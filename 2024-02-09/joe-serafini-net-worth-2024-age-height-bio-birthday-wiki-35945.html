Joe Serafini is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on January 22, 1998 in Pennsylvania, Pennsylvania, United States. Television actor who rose to fame after being cast as Seb on the Disney Plus series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.

Both he and Olivia Rodrigo were cast on High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.

Serafini is from Bethel Park, Pennsylvania, a suburb of Pittsburgh. He was a “mini star” at the Pittsburgh CLO Academy (Pittsburgh Civic Light Opera), where he earned a Gene Kelly Award scholarship.
