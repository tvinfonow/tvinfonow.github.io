Whitlock was born in South Bend, Indiana. He attended college at Southwest Minnesota State University in Marshall, Minnesota, where he enrolled earned a football scholarship and studied theater. Injuries led him to stop playing football and focus on acting. After graduating in 1976, he moved to San Francisco and joined the American Conservatory Theater.

SMSU now offers the Isiah Whitlock Jr. Endowed Scholarship. He was SMSU’s commencement speaker in 1999 and guest artist for the school’s celebration of Black History Month in 2007.
