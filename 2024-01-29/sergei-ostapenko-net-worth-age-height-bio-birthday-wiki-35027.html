Sergei Ostapenko is one of the most popular and richest Footballer who was born on February 23, 1986 in Almaty, Kazakh SSR, Kazakhstan. Sergei Sergeyevich Ostapenko (Russian: Sergei Ostapenko ) (born 23 February, 1986) is an ex- Kazakh footballer, whose previous club consisted of FC Astana.

The summer of 2014 saw Ostapenko changed his affiliation to FC Astana to FC Kaisar, returning to Astana in April 2015 following his Kaisar contract was up.
