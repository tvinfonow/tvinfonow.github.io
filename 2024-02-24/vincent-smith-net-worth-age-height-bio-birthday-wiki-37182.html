Smith was the very first footballer who has ever played in Michigan in history to have scored passing, rushing and receiving touchdowns in one game. Smith is the very first Division I FBS player to achieve this feat since 2009. Smith’s touchdown pass was 17 yards towards sophomore wide player Drew Dileo. In the 2011 season, Smith scored 296 rushing miles and 142 receiving yards.
