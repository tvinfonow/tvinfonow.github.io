Cecilia Veronica “CeCe” Peniston (/s iː ˈ s iː ˈ p ɛ n ɪ s t ən / ; born September 6, 1969) is an American singer and former beauty queen. In the early 1990s, she scored five number one hits on the U.S. Billboard Hot Dance Music/Club Play. Her signature song “Finally” reached the number 5 spot on the Hot 100 and number 2 in UK Top 75.

Her music career began in January 1991, when Felipe “DJ Wax Dawg” Delgado, her friend and a record producer based also in Phoenix, asked Peniston to record back-up vocals for Tonya Davis, a black female rapper known as Overweight Pooch after her childhood nickname.
