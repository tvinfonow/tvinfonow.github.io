Top Facts about Steve McNair

  • Steve McNair was an NFL quarterback from 1995-2007.
  • He played for the Houston Oilers/Tennessee Titans and Baltimore Ravens.
  • McNair was a three-time Pro Bowler and Co-MVP in 2003.
  • He led the Titans to Super Bowl XXXIV in 2000.
  • McNair had over 31,000 career passing yards and 174 touchdowns.
  • He also rushed for over 3,500 yards and scored 37 rushing touchdowns.
  • McNair tragically died at age 36 in a murder-suicide in 2009.
  • He was posthumously elected to the Tennessee Sports Hall of Fame in 2019.
  • McNair’s jersey number (9) has been retired by the Titans franchise.
  • His legacy continues through the Steve McNair Foundation, supporting youth education initiatives.
