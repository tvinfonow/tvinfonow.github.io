Mikey Craig Biography

Mikey Craig is one of the most popular and richest Celebrity who was born on February 15, 1960 in Hammersmith, Hammersmith, United Kingdom. Michael Emile “Mikey” Craig (born 15 February 1960) is an English musician and DJ of Jamaican descent, best known as the bassist of the pop/soul/new wave group Culture Club.

Craig was an executive producer, in the mid-1990s, for his own dance label, SLAMM records, generating dance hits by artists like Mr. & Mrs. Smith and Mankey.
