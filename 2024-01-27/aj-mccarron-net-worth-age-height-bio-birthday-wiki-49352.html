AJ McCarron Biography

AJ McCarron is one of the most popular and richest Football Player who was born on September 13, 1990 in Mobile, Alabama, United States. Quarterback who helped lead the University of Alabama to back to back National Championships in 2011 and 2012. He was selected 164th overall in the 2014 NFL Draft by the Cincinnati Bengals.

Under head coach Nick Saban, McCarron helped lead one of the most dominant periods in Alabama football history.

While at Alabama, he began a much publicized relationship with Katherine Webb, winner of the 2012 Miss Alabama pageant. The couple got married in 2014; they had a son named Tripp in 2016 and a son named Cash in 2018.
