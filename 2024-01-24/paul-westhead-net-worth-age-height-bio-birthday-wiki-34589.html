Top Facts about Paul Westhead

  • Coached Loyola Marymount to NCAA Elite Eight in 1990.
  • Won NBA championship with Los Angeles Lakers in 1980.
  • Known for fast-paced, high-scoring “system” of basketball.
  • Coached multiple WNBA teams, including Phoenix Mercury and Seattle Storm.
  • Inducted into the Women’s Basketball Hall of Fame in 2018.
  • Holds record for most points scored by a team in a single NCAA tournament game (LMU, 149).
  • Coached George Mason University men’s basketball team from 1997-2001.
  • Has coached professional teams overseas in Japan and South Korea.
  • Played college basketball at St. Joseph’s University under coach Jack Ramsay.
  • Was head coach at La Salle University from 1970-79 before moving to NBA coaching ranks.
