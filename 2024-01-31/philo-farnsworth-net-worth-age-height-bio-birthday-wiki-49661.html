Philo Farnsworth Biography

Philo Farnsworth is one of the most popular and richest Engineer who was born on August 19, 1906 in Beaver, Utah, United States. Electrical engineer who created several key components that made the first televisions possible. His firm, the Farnsworth Television and Radio Corporation, produced his electronic television system commercially from 1938 to 195.

He based a lot of his research on the work done by John Logie Baird, another great contributor to television technology.

He grew up near the town of Beaver in southwestern Utah, his father a follower of the Brigham Young, who lived in a log cabin built by his own father.
