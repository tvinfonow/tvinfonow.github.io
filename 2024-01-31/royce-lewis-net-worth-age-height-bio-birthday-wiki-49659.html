Royce Lewis Biography

Royce Lewis is one of the most popular and richest Baseball Player who was born on June 5, 1999 in Aliso Viejo, California, United States. Royce Oliver Lewis, born June 5, 1999, is an American professional baseball shortstop with the Minnesota Twins. The Twins drafted him first in the 2017 Major League Baseball Draft.

Lewis was promoted to Fort Myers Miracle of Class A-Advanced Florida State League on July 14, 2018. MLB.com ranked Lewis 10th among prospects in baseball on July 26, 2018. Lewis finished his time in Fort Myers at.255, with five home runs as well as 21 RBIs in 46 games. Lewis and teammate Alex Kirilloff were named to the Midwest League postseason All-Star team following the 2018 season. Lewis was also named MVP of the Midwest League.

Lewis was a top prospect for the 2017 Major League Baseball Draft. To play college baseball, he committed to the University of California at Irvine. After batting.377 as a senior with four home runs, and 25 stolen bases, he was named California’s Baseball Gatorade player of the year in 2017.
