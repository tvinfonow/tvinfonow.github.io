Albert, Prince Consort Biography

Albert, Prince Consort is one of the most popular and richest Prince who was born on August 26, 1819 in Germany, Germany. Remembered best as the Prince Consort to Queen Victoria of England, Albert is also famous for his progressive ideas and for his positions as Chancellor of the University of Cambridge and President of the Society of Arts.

His oldest child and first daughter, Victoria Princess Royal, was born in 1840.

He was born in Schloss Rosenau, Germany, to Louise of Saxe-Gotha-Altenburg and Duke Ernest III of Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld. His marriage to England’s Queen Victoria resulted in five daughters and four sons.
