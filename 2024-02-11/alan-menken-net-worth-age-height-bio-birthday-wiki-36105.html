Alan Menken Biography

Alan Menken is one of the most popular and richest Composer who was born on July 22, 1949 in New Rochelle, New York State, United States. Composer who wrote the scores for several beloved Disney films, including 1989’s The Little Mermaid. He won two Academy Awards for Best Original Song and Best Original Score for Beauty and the Beast and Pocahontas.

His first major professional production was God Bless You, Mr. Rosewater from 1979. He based it on the Kurt Vonnegut Jr. novel of the same name.

He was born into a Jewish family to Judith and Norman Menken, who was a dentist. He married Janis Roswick in 1972, with whom he fathered two daughters.

Who wrote the most Disney songs?

Alan Irwin Menken (born July 22, 1949) is an American composer, best known for his scores and songs for films produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. His scores and songs for The Little Mermaid (1989), Beauty and the Beast (1991), Aladdin (1992), and Pocahontas (1995) have each won him two Academy Awards.
