Dory Funk Jr. Biography

Dory Funk Jr. is one of the most popular and richest Wrestler who was born on February 3, 1941 in Hammond, Indiana, United States. Also known as Hoss Funk, he was inducted into the NWA Hall of Fame in 2006, and the WWE Hall of Fame in 2009.

After retiring from wrestling he opened up a wrestling school, where he has taught wrestlers such as Mickie James.

He married Jimmie Funk in 1960, and Marti Funk in 1989, and raised five children between the two.
