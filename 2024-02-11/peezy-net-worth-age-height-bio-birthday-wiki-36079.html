Who is peezy from Detroit?

A Detroit rapper and hip-hop artist, Peezy, born Phillip Glen-Earl Peaks, is part of the Motor City’s Team Eastside rap collective. Peezy began making waves in 2015 with the release of his third album, Mud Sweat & Tears.
