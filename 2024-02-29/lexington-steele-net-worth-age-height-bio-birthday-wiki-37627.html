Lexington Steele Biography

Lexington Steele is one of the most popular and richest Stockbroker who was born on November 28, 1969 in Morristown, New Jersey, United States. Clifton Todd Britt, better known as Lexington Steele (born November 28, 1969), is an American pornographic actor and director, who also owns Mercenary motion Pictures and Black Viking Pictures Inc.

Steele was married to Vanessa Blue, a porn actress and director. They appeared together on Playboy TV in his two-season series Lex in the City and in many movies before ending their relationship in June 2006.

During his time studying African-American studies, Steele developed an awareness of American politics and African-American rights, while in 2008 he expressed his support for Barack Obama’s presidential campaign.

In 2008, Steele and Blue filed federal lawsuits against each other over the ownership of numerous videos that first appeared under the Mercenary Pictures label, which were settled in March 2009 after meeting with an alternative dispute resolution jurist.

Steele graduated from Syracuse University in 1993 and became a trainee stock broker at M S Farrell’s brokerage firm. After obtaining his Series 7 trading license, Steele moved to Oppenheimer Financial at the World Trade Center. He stated that he would be in the buildings and have been a victim to the September 11 2001 attacks if he had not left his stockbroker job.
