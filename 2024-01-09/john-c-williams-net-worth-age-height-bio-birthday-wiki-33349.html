John C. Williams Biography

John C. Williams is one of the most popular and richest Economist who was born on June 12, 1962 in Sacramento, California, United States.

Williams earned an A.B. with high distinction from the University of California at Berkeley in 1984; a Master’s of Science with distinction in economics from the London School of Economics in 1989, and completed a Ph.D. in Economics at Stanford University in 1994.

Williams began his career in 1994 as an economist at the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System. He joined the Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco in 2002. Prior to becoming the president, he was the executive vice president and director of research for the San Francisco bank. He took office as President and Chief Executive Officer of the Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco on March 1, 2011.
