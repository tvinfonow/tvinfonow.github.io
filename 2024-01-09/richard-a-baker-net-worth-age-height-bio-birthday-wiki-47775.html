Richard A. Baker Biography

Richard A. Baker is one of the most popular and richest Business Executive who was born on November 27, 1965 in United States, United States.

He began his career working for his father, a mall developer and principal of National Realty & Development Corp, after graduating from the Cornell University School of Hotel Administration. In 2006, he purchased Lord & Taylor, America’s longest continually operating luxury department store from Federated Department Stores, borrowing to finance nearly all of the deal. He has served as Chairman of Lord & Taylor since 2006.

In 2008, he bought the Hudson’s Bay Company, North America’s oldest company (established by English royal charter in 1670), becoming its Governor (company chairman). On November 1, 2017, he took over as interim CEO of the company when Jerry Storch’s resignation became effective. Hudson’s Bay Company had gone public in November 2012 and was then listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange.
