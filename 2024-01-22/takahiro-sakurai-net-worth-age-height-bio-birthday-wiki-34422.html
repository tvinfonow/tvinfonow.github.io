Who is the VA of Sasori?

Takahiro Sakurai (櫻井 孝宏; born June 13, 1974 in Okazaki, Aichi, Japan) is a Japanese voice actor. He’s known for voicing: Cloud Strife in Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children, Sasori in Naruto: Shippūden and Suzaku Kururugi in Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion.
