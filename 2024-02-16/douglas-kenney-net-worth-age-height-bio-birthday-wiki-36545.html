Douglas Kenney Biography

Douglas Kenney is one of the most popular and richest Comedian who was born on December 10, 1946 in West Palm Beach, Florida, United States. Writer and actor who created an entirely new form of satirical literature when he founded the National Lampoon magazine in 1970. He also co-wrote and produced the classic comedy films Animal House and Caddyshack.

His brand of satire went on to influence future popular comedians like Jon Stewart.

He grew up in West Palm Beach, Florida. He was briefly married to Alexandra Appleton Garcia-Mata from 1970 until 1973.

    What happened Doug Kenney?

    Kenney died on August 27, 1980, aged 33, after falling from a 35-foot cliff called the Hanapepe Lookout. Police found his car the following day; three days later, Kenney’s body was discovered between two jagged rocks at the bottom of the cliff. His death was classified as accidental by Kauai police.
