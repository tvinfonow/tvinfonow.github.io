DJ Paul Elstak Biography

DJ Paul Elstak is one of the most popular and richest Dutch DJ who was born on January 14, 1966 in The Hague, South Holland, Netherlands. Paul Roger Elstak (known professionally as DJ Paul Elstak; born 14 January 1966 in The Hague) is a Dutch hardcore/gabber as well as a happy DJ as well as record producer. He was previously using the full title to make the happy, hardcore label and DJ Paul for hardcore gabber However, after he launched Offensive Records in 2001, the DJ started using both names for gabber.

He left in 2001. quit Mid-Town in 2001 and Rotterdam Records to start a new record label with Rige Entertainment, called Offensive Records.

The year 1995 was the time he dropped his first DJ Paul Elstak single, “Life Is Like a Dance”. After the release of this single several commercial releases were released. “Rainbow in the Sky” was the most popular of them all, selling more than 50,000 units in 1995. Many of his singles made into the Dutch Top 40 in the mid-1990s. Two years later, Elstak took the choice to step away from the dance industry and focus on the release of gabber.
