Top Facts about Charlie Berens

  • Charlie Berens was born April 27, 1987 into a Catholic family in Wisconsin.
  • He attended Marquette University High School and later the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where he studied broadcast journalism.
  • Berens rose to fame after working on MTV News show Choose or Lose, hosting the comedic news show Nightcap and winning a Regional Emmy Award.
  • In 2017, he created the weekly comedic video series Manitowoc Minute.
  • Berens authored the bookThe Midwest Survival Guide: How We Talk, Love, Work, Drink, and Eat…Everything with Ranch (2021).
  • He launched a Kickstarter crowdfunding campaign for the card-based party game Card Sale in September 2022 and raised three times their original goal of $11,700.
  • Berens gave the keynote speech at the UW-Madison Winter commencement in December 2022 and held a book signing afterwards.
  • In June 2020, he launched The Cripescast, a podcast interviewing Midwest-based musicians, artists, comedians and creators.
  • He was married to TV host and former Miss Wisconsin USA Alex Wehrley from 2015-2020.
  • He currently resides in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
