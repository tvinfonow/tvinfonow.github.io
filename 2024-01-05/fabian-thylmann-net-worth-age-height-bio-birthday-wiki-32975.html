Fabian Thylmann Biography

Fabian Thylmann is one of the most popular and richest Entrepreneur who was born on June 5, 1978 in Düsseldorf, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. Fabian Thylmann (born 5 June 1978) is an entrepreneur who hails from Germany who was the founder and the managing partner of Internet pornography company Manwin (now MindGeek). In October 2013, he disposed of part of the business that was at the time the biggest operator of pornography worldwide. Since then, he is an angel investor in young Startups situated in Brussels, Belgium where he also operates a coworking and incubator space, SN-Cube.
