Gustavo Díaz Ordaz Biography

Gustavo Díaz Ordaz is one of the most popular and richest World Leader who was born on March 12, 1911 in Ciudad Serdán, Puebla, Mexico. A part of the Institutional Revolutionary Party, he served as president of Mexico between 1964 and 1970. While he was widely criticized for his involvement during the 1968 Tlatelolco mass murder of students, he nevertheless did accept the Treaty of Tlatelolco which prohibited Latin American countries from possessing nuclear weapons.
