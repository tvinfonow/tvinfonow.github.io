Leandro Isaac (born February 2nd 1961) is an Filipino Basketball player who was a former sportscaster and director of basketball for the Blackwater Elite in the Philippine Basketball Association.

He was a retired player in the PBA following the 1995 season.

He led them to several titles including the NCRAA championship in 2007. After a season coaching Red Bull, in 2010 he once again coached the Chiefs, this time, in the NCAA for two seasons.

Isaac took over as Mapua’s head coach prior to the 2007 season. He led the Cardinals to back-to-back Final 4 slots wherein during both appearances they were at #4. His records with them were 6-6 and 9-5, respectively.

Prior to the start of the 1992 PBA season the player was transferred to Pepsi in exchange for Tonichi Yturri, and then to Shell Rimula-X in exchange for Gido Babilonia and Leo Austria. He was instrumental in helping the Oilers to win their 1992 First Conference championship against the San Miguel Beermen. The team remained with him through the season of 1993.
