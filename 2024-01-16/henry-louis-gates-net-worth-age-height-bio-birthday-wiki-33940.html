What happened to Henry Louis Gates?

At the age of 14, Gates was injured playing touch football, fracturing the ball and socket joint of his hip, resulting in a slipped capital femoral epiphysis. The injury was misdiagnosed by a physician, who told Gates’ mother that his problem was psychosomatic.

Why does Dr Gates use a cane?

A white doctor misdiagnosed the injury as psychosomatic after Gates told him he wanted to become a doctor, Gates wrote in a New York Times article, “About Men: A Giant Step,” in 1990. As a result of the injury, Gates walks with a cane and his right leg is more than 2 inches shorter than his left.
