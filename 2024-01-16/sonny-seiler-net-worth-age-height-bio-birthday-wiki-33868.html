Is Uga the bulldog still alive?

Uga VII passed away unexpectedly on Nov. 19, 2009 of heart-related causes. It was the Thursday before the final home game of the 2009 season and the Bulldogs did not have a live mascot at the game.

Does Uga mascot get paid?

Following Uga’s death, the Seiler family got a second dog – Uga II – and have been sharing their pets with the university football team and fans ever since. They do not get paid for lending their dogs to the team, although the pets do enjoy the perks of sponsorships.
