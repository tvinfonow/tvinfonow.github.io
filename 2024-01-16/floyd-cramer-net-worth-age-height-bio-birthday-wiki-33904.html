Did Floyd Cramer write Last Date?

“Last Date” is a 1960 instrumental written and performed by Floyd Cramer. It exemplifies the “slip note” style of piano playing that Cramer made popular. It peaked at number 11 on the country chart and at number two on the Hot 100 behind “Are You Lonesome Tonight?” by Elvis Presley.
