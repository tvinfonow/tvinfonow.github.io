Nick Lowery Biography

Nick Lowery is one of the most popular and richest Football Player who was born on May 27, 1956 in Munich, Germany. Dominic Gerald Lowery (born May 27, 1956), nicknamed Nick the Kick, is a former American football placekicker. In his career he played for the New England Patriots, Kansas City Chiefs, and New York Jets. Lowery was selected to the Pro Bowl three times and when he retired was ranked first in field goal percentage and also had the most field goals in NFL history. As of 2018 he was 16th on the National Football League’s list of all-time scoring leaders, and is the Chiefs’ all-time leading scorer, with 1,466 points in his 14 seasons with the club.
