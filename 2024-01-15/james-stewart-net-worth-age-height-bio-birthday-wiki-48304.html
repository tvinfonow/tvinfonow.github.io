James Stewart Biography

James Stewart is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on May 20, 1908 in Indiana, Pennsylvania, United States. Earned a Best Actor Academy Award for his role in the 1940 film The Philadelphia Story. He was also nominated for an Oscar for his starring role in the 1946 Christmas film It’s A Wonderful Life. He gave memorable performances in several Alfred Hitchcock‘s films, including: Rear Window, Vertigo, and The Man Who Knew Too Much.

He co-starred in the 1959 film Anatomy of a Murder with Lee Remick.

He had four children with Gloria Hatrick McLean, whom he was married to from 1949 until her death in 1994.

What were Jimmy Stewart's last words?

Three years before Stewart’s death in 1997, his wife of 45 years, Gloria Hatrick McLean died of lung cancer. Her loss was noted to be a heavy blow to Stewart and on his deathbed, surrounded by family and friends, the last words Stewart spoke were, “I’m going to go be with Gloria now.”
