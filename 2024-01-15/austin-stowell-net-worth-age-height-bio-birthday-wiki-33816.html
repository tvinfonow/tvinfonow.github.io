Austin Stowell Biography

Austin Stowell is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on December 24, 1984 in Kensington, Connecticut, United States. Actor who played Dalton Joiner in the 2013 romantic drama Love and Honor. He also played Kyle Connellan in Dolphin Tale and Dolphin Tale 2. In 2019, he began starring in Hulu’s Catch-22.

He appeared in Bridge of Spies starring Tom Hanks.

He graduated from Berlin High School in 2003 with the intention of pursuing a professional career in acting. Upon acceptance at the University of Connecticut in Storrs, Connecticut, he studied with the Department of Dramatic Arts, a division of the School of Fine Arts. He performed in several productions with the Connecticut Repertory Theatre, including Julius Caesar, It Can’t Happen Here, and As You Like It.

Stowell graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 2007.

His father was a steelworker and his mother was a schoolteacher. In 2015, he began a relationship with Nina Dobrev, but the two broke up in early 2016.
