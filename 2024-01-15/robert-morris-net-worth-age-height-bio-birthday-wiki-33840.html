Was Robert Morris a signer of the Declaration of Independence?

What did Robert Morris do for the Declaration of Independence?

Morris signed the new U. S. Constitution, one of only two signers of the Declaration of Independence to sign all three basic founding documents—the Declaration, the Articles of Confederation and the U.S. Constitution. (The other signer of all three documents was Roger Sherman of Connecticut.)
