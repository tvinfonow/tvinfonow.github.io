PD James Biography

PD James is one of the most popular and richest Novelist who was born on August 3, 1920 in England, United Kingdom. A English criminal writer who is most well recognized for her popular detective series that features the poetic detective Adam Dalgliesh. Her non-serial novels include Innocent Blood (1980), The Children of Men (1992) as well as Death Comes to Pemberley (2011).

Her book was the inspiration for the film of the same name, Children of Men, which was a film starring Clive Owen.

She was raised living in Oxford, England. Her daughters were Claire Jane and Jane, along with her husband The Dr. Ernest Connor White.
