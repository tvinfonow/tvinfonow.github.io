Harry Strauss Zelnick (born June 26, 1957) is an American businessman. Harry Strauss Zelnick was born on June 26, 1957, in Boston. He was the second-oldest of six children of Elsa (née Strauss) and Allan Zelnick (died in 2013) and grew up in South Orange, New Jersey. Strauss Zelnick graduated from Columbia High School in 1975 and studied psychology and English at Wesleyan University from 1975 until 1979, obtaining a Bachelor of Arts degree with honors. During his time at Wesleyan, he was the national public relations director for the Coalition of Independent College and University Students (COPUS). Zelnick simultaneously enrolled at Harvard Business School and Harvard Law School in 1979, obtaining a Master of Business Administration degree from the former in 1982 and a Juris Doctor from the latter the year after.
