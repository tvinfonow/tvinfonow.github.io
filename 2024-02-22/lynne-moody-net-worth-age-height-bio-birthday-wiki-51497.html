Top Facts about Lynne Moody

  • Lynne Moody is an American actress born on February 17, 1946.
  • She has appeared in over 70 TV shows and movies.
  • Moody played Tracy Curtis Taylor on “That’s My Mama” (1974-75).
  • She was the first African-American woman to appear on “Little House on the Prairie.”
  • Moody starred as Dr. Gail Lucas on “Knots Landing” (1988-1990).
  • She won a Daytime Emmy Award for her role in “Anatomy of a Seduction.”
  • Moody has also worked as a voice-over artist for animated series.

  • Moody is an advocate for breast cancer awareness and education.
