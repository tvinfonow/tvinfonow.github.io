Devin McGlamery Biography

Devin McGlamery is one of the most popular and richest Musician who was born on June 9, 1982 in Valdosta, Georgia, United States.

Jon Devin McGlamery (born June 9, 1982) is an American Christian musician, who is a GMA Dove Award-winning artist, and the current lead vocalist of Ernie Haase & Signature Sound. He is a former tenor member of Karen Peck and New River and former leading vocalist for the Dixie Melody Boys. He released Love is a Verb, a studio album, in 2013 with Stow Town Records.

McGlamery was first a member of the Dixie Melody Boys, from 2000 until 2004, when he left to join Karen Peck and New River during a stint from 2004 until 2009. He became a member of Ernie Haase & Signature Sound, in 2010, where he is still their lead vocalist.
